Description

TerpSync is an app that allows you to easily find events on campus by having everything just a tap away. Find what events are happening around you. See what your favorite student organization has coming up.


If you are a student organization leader at UMD...

Contact terpsync@gmail.com to request an account so that you can create your own events. This is a great way to market your events, whether it be to keep your current members updated or to reach out to new members.


Features

Available now:
  • View upcoming events in either a list or map representation
  • Filter events by organization, building, and free/paid events
  • Create your own events (for student organizations)

Upcoming:
  • Favorite clubs and events
  • Notifications for events
  • Student organization profile page

Currently only available at University of Maryland, College Park
