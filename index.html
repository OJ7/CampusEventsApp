Description

TerpSync is an app that allows you to easily discover events on campus by having everything just a tap away. Discover what events are happening around you. See what your favorite student organization has coming up.


If you are a student organization leader at UMD...

Contact terpsync@gmail.com to request an account so that you can create your own events. This is a great way to market your events, whether it be to keep your current members in sync or to reach out to new members.

Instructions:

  • Signing In - Click on the settings icon and click "Sign in". You can then change your username/password to whatever you want from settings.
  • Add Events - Click on either the list icon or open up a list from the building markers. There should be a (+) icon at the bottom right which you can click to fill out a form. Events show up instantly after submitting events.
  • Edit/Delete Events - Clicking on an event you created from any list. You will be presented with an option to edit/delete the event.

Features

Available now:
  • View upcoming events in either a list or map representation
  • Filter events by organization, building, and free/paid events
  • Create your own events (for student organizations)
Upcoming:
  • Favorite clubs and events
  • Notifications for events
  • Student organization profile page

Currently only available at University of Maryland, College Park
